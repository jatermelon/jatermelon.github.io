Common Fate Principal

Posted on 2018-10-09 | In Design
Words count in article: 291 | Reading time ≈ 1

共同命运

The principle of common fate is one of a number of principles referred to as Gestalt principles of perception. It asserts that elements that move together in a common direction are perceived as a single group or chunk, and are interpreted as being more related than elements that move at different times or in different directions. For example, a row of randomly arranged Xs and Os that is stationary is naturally grouped by similarity, Xs with Xs, and Os with Os. However, if certain elements in the row move in one direction, and other elements move in the opposite direction, elements are grouped by their common motion and direction.

虽然不是设计师，但一直以来都被设计的美感所吸引。恰巧在图书馆看到《设计的法则》便借来一读。很享受整个阅读过程，被很多设计的法则以及人的心理认知的理念所吸引。尤其被喜欢这条共同命运原则。，读完一两天后还在津津有味的回想着。

你是圆圈，我是方块，而他是三角。我们看起来是彼此互不相同的毫无交集的个体，然而共同的命运却把我们联系在了一起，使各不相同的我们成为了同类。所谓志同道合，惺惺相惜，便是如此了。
